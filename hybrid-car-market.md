<p><p>The hybrid car market comprises three main types: Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs), Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs), and Electric Vehicles (EVs). HEVs combine a conventional internal combustion engine with an electric motor, optimizing fuel efficiency without needing external charging. PHEVs also feature both engine types but can be charged from an external source, allowing for longer electric-only driving ranges. EVs operate solely on electric power, relying on battery storage and charging infrastructure, representing the shift towards fully electric mobility solutions.</p></p>
