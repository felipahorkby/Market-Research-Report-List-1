<p><p>The laminated steel container market is segmented into two main types: 2-piece cans and 3-piece cans. Two-piece cans are manufactured from a single steel sheet, providing a seamless structure that enhances durability and aesthetic appeal, often used for beverages. In contrast, three-piece cans are constructed from three separate steel components—two cylindrical bodies and a lid—allowing for flexibility in size and design, making them suitable for various food and industrial applications. Each type has distinct advantages in terms of production efficiency and product safety.</p></p>
